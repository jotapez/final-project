Family Law

At Galbally & O’Bryan we understand that injury claims are stressful and that the compensation you receive has a direct impact on your financial security.

Over the years, our dedicated group of personal injury lawyers, led by Nicholas O’Bryan, has successfully resolved thousands of claims of varying complexity including claims related to transport and car accident injury, WorkCover, public liability and medical negligence. We help you to understand your compensation rights and entitlements by providing clear legal advice. We work to maximise your compensation entitlement and to resolve your claim as quickly as possible in a cost effective manner. Each personal injury case is managed by a Partner of the firm. Some of our lawyers are also medically qualified; a significant advantage when contesting medical issues linked to entitlements. ‘No win – no charge’ We provide access to legal assistance regardless of your financial position and generally act on a ‘no win – no charge’ basis. We offer free initial phone advice and, if we think we can help you, a free first consultation.
